About the Creator

My name is Phyllis and I am the creator of this content. Ottawa is not the biggest city in Canada, but if you've never spent time in Ottawa, then I think making the trip to this iconic city is definitely worth doing at least once. You can go to the recommended places on the #attractions tab, it is a great city to explore, and there are plenty of things to see and do. From historical sites to cultural attractions to the must-see Parliament Hill, Ottawa will leave you feeling proud to be Canadian and more informed about the country we call home.
